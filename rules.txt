Pickle Game Rules

- web app
- Multiplayer
- more player the bigger the board
- the board consist of cells, each contain a random letter
- the players get one minute, ea player must find a word in that time
- player is out of the game if they can not find a word in the minute:
- each player gets a point per unique cell played
- invalid if word not in the dictionary
- each player has a colour
- each cell played turns to their colour
- introduce a blocking rule based on player colours
- game finished once all tiles are coloured
- player with the most points wins
